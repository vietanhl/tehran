Abo-o-Atash Park

Didar St., Tehran 11369, Iran

An excellent tourist Park in Tehran

Ab-o-Atash Park is one of the most attractive places in Tehran that includes tons of interesting areas. A great choice for walking, playing as well as having a special food in one of the several different type of restaurants over there.
